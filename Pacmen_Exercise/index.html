[ Pacmen Exercise ]

This assignment was testing our ability to manipulate the DOM by adding Pacmen images and having them start at random positions with random initialized velocities. Each press of the "Add PacMan" button creates a PacMan element, storing and managing them in an array. Once you have your desired amount of prisoners, you can have them run amok your screen by pressing the Start Game button, where they will bounce off the edges of the viewport until the page is refreshed.

The chaos is managed by an update() function that iterates over the array and applies their randomized velocity used to move the element, then calls itself after a moment to create the movement of the Pacman. Once the Pacman reaches the edge of the screen, it reverses it's direction while it faces and travels the other way.
